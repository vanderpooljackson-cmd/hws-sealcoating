Why Oil-Based

The Difference is in the Sealer

Most companies use cheap water-based sealant that fades within a season. We use premium oil-based sealant that bonds with asphalt and stays beautiful for years.

  • Penetrates deep into asphalt — not just a surface coating
  • The oil-based sealant leaves a nice jet matte black finish
  • Superior protection against gasoline, oil & UV rays
  • Restores flexibility — prevents cracking & potholes
  • Better adhesion in cold and wet climates
